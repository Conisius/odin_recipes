Description

Scrambled Eggs are one of the easiest and tasty dished you can make.

Depending on number of eggs it can bolster your protein intake really quickly and easy.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • Â¼ cup cottage cheese
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh chives, or to taste (Optional)
  • ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour beaten eggs into the skillet; let cook undisturbed until the bottom of the eggs begins to firm, 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Stir cottage cheese and chives into eggs and season with black pepper.
  4. Step 4: Cook and stir until eggs are nearly set, 3 to 4 minutes more.
