Description

Made with love and ideal for quick and easy protein intake

Perfect for a quick snack in the car going to the gym or after exercise.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 ½ cups quick-cooking oats
  • ¼ cup wheat germ
  • ¼ cup ground flax seed
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla-flavored whey protein powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 bananas
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line an 8x8-inch baking dish with aluminum foil, letting foil hang down over the sides of the pan; spray pan with cooking spray.
  2. Step 2: Stir oats, wheat germ, flax seed, protein powder, cinnamon, and salt together in a bowl. Mash bananas in a separate large bowl and stir 1/4 cup Jif® peanut butter, honey, and vanilla extract into bananas, mixing well. Stir dry ingredients into banana mixture and spread into the prepared baking dish.
  3. Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Spread 1/4 cup natural peanut butter over top.
  4. Step 4: Continue baking until lightly browned on the edges and set, 10 more minutes. Let cool in the pan and lift bars onto a work surface using aluminum foil as handles. Cut into bars, wrap in plastic wrap, and store in refrigerator.
